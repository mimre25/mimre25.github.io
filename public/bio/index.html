Bio

Martin Imre is currently working as Data Scientist/Software Developer at greentec development gmbh developing parts of an automatic fault detection engine for solar plants. He received his PhD in Computer Science from the University of Notre Dame in May 2020 researching high-performance data analysis methods for scientific data visualization. Before his PhD studies he received an MSc and BSc in Software Engineering in 2016 and 2014 respectively.
Besides teaching and research experience, he has collected professional experience during internships at the MCS Department at Argonne National Lab (Lemont, IL May - July 2018), the Secure Systems and Software Lab at University of California Irvine (July - Sept. 2015), as Software Engineer at Project-A GmbH & Co KG (Berlin July - Sept. 2014), and during part-time employment at the VRVis Research Center (Vienna, Feb. - June 2015, Oct. 2015 - June 2016)
